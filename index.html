Shortcuts

press:

  • 'escape' to switch on or switch off calculator
  • '0' ... '9' for input numbers
  • '+', '-', '*' and '/' for addition, subtraction, multiplication or division accordingly. Also you may use these buttons for getting intermediate results
  • 'enter' if you need output result
  • 'backspace' if you want to correct data you input
  • '.' when you want to use decimal fraction
  • 'end'in a case you wish to change sign of your number form '-' to '+' and vice versa
  • 'space' for clearing display
